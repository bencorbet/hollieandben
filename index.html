GETTING THERE

some tips & advice

Cripps Barn is located in the Costwolds village of Bibury, which a fifteen minute drive from Cirencester. It is easily accessible by car, train and plane.

  • car

    Postcode: GL7 5BA
    GPS co-ordinates: N51° 46.039’ W1° 52.573’

    Cirencester, 15 minutes
    Hereford, 1 hour 15 minutes
    London, 1 hour 55 minutes
    Manchester, 2 hours 54 minutes
    Guernsey, 1 hour 20 minutes (and a boat/plane or very good swimming skills).

  • train

    The closest railway station is Kemble, which is 4.5 miles from Cirencester.

    Alternatively, Charlbury station is 18.2 miles away and Swindon Station. 23.7 miles.

    Please be aware that the taxi rank at Kemble Station often isn’t manned. Taxi’s will need to be booked ahead of time.

  • plane

    Birmingham Airport, 1hr 20 minutes.
    FlyBe from Guernsey, Paris and Glasgow.

    Bristol Airport, 1hr 20 minutes
    Aurigny from Guernsey, Easyjet from Paris and Glasgow.

    Please let us know if you are arriving the day before the wedding as we may be able to help with transportfrom the airport!

  • shuttle

    A bus will be put on between local Cirencester hotels and the venue, so you can kick back, relax and make sure you have your dancing shoes on late into the night! if you would like to take part, please mark your interest within your RSVP. Details of departure times and locations will be provided closer to the big day.

  • where to stay

  • save

    6 miles away, rooms from £40.00

    8 miles away, room from £36.00

  • splurge

  • camp

    Camping the night of the wedding is welcome and free at Cripps Barn. Booking is not necessary, but please let us know if you intend to camp.
    Tents can be set up from midday the day of the wedding, with departure by 10:00am the next morning.
    Please note that there aren’t facilities as such.There is a stand-pipe for water and when the party ends and the barn is locked up, the front door will be left open for access to the bathrooms.
    Campers, caravans and cars for ten users can be parked at the car end of the car park.

FAQ

for all of those little questions

  • what time are festivities starting?

    The ceremony will begin at 2 o’clock, but please arrive before this to give you time to be seated and get settled.

  • i am flying in, help!

    Cripps Barn is located about an hours drive from Bristol and Birmingham airports. Cars can be hired at both for around £20-40 for three days.

  • what is the parking situation?

    Cripps Barn have a pretty generous free parking lot.

  • what accommodations are availiable?

    There are lots of local B&B’s, pubs and hotels to choose from, ranging from around £30-40 a night (Premier Inn and Travelodge) to £140+. If you fancy camping, camping is free at Cripps Barn on the night of the wedding. Please get in touch for more information, but be advised that facilities are limited.

  • i want to drink and preferably not drive.

    If you are staying in Cirencester over night, a minibus will be collecting guests from the town and returning them after all the fun has finished. Alternatively, there are a few local taxi companies. A taxi from Cripps Barn to the surrounding hotels would be between £8-20.

  • minibus from/to my hotel? how do I sign up?

    Please let us know when you RSVP and we can give you the details!

  • what do I wear?

    The event is semi-formal, but anything you wish to wear that is in good taste we are sure will be okay. The bride requests that you refrain from wearing a white or a wedding dress, that’s about it.

  • avoid dressing like the bridal party?

    If it concerns you, avoid blush pink. Otherwise, if it doesn't concern you, it does not concern us.

  • what can I expect, generally?

    Ceremony, confetti, amazing food, pimms, dancing, bonfires and cakes.

  • what's up with the bar?

    Red, white and rose wine will be served during dinner, as well as Pimms and cider during the cocktail hour and prosecco to toast. There is a fully stocked bar in the barn which takes both card and cash payments.

  • can I take pictures?

    Of course! We all know how much the bride loves photos. All we ask is that you please avoid blocking our lovely photographers Marta and Artur (unless you’re blocking them with your lovely smiley faces of course).

  • what about social media?

    If you use Snapchat, feel free to use our super cool custom geofilter (and send the snaps to us too!). If you use Instagram, please tag us using #benollie so that we can look back on the craziness while eating pizza in bed.
